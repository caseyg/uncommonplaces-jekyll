I am in New Orleans for the [Chase Competition][1] finals. We present on Tuesday. In the mean time, we are polishing off our presentation and enjoying the local atmosphere. I did not make it to Jazz Fest yesterday because I was totally beat between an all-nighter on Thursday and catching the flight on Saturday at 7am. I am enjoying hanging out in this city. It smells so sweet here!
